Amy Wei

Hi! I’m an undergraduate student at the University of Michigan majoring in Computer Science and minoring in Mathematics. I’m currently a junior, with an expected graduation of May 2026. I am interested in pursuing a PhD to go into a career in research, with interests in software engineering and security.

In summer 2023 and 2024, I was a Research Assistant at Carnegie Mellon University through the Research Experience for Undergraduates in Software Engineering (REUSE) program. I worked with PhD student Matthew Davis and professors Joshua Sunshine and Brad Myers to build NaNofuzz, a testing tool for TypeScript programs that can be installed in VS Code.

I have taught as an Instructional Aide for Discrete Mathematics (EECS 203) since Winter 2024 in the U-M Computer Science and Engineering Department.
